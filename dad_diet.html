🚫 Foods Dad Must Avoid

  • 🍟 All fried foods — puri, bhatura, pakoda, samosa
  • 🍬 Sugar & sweets — mithai, gulab jamun, ladoo, cold drinks
  • 🥐 Bakery / Maida — white bread, biscuits, naan
  • 🧴 Excess oil — max 10–15 ml/day (use mustard or olive)
  • 🧂 High-sodium pickles, papads, processed snacks
  • 🍦 Full-fat dairy — butter, cream, full-fat paneer
